Case Study #1

The Texas Collection of Comedias Sueltas and Spanish Theater Database

The Texas Collection of Comedias Sueltas and Spanish Theater contains over 15,000 dramatic works from the Spanish theater published in Spain from the 17th through the early 20th century.

This case study presents a series of interactive visualizations aiming to provide users with new ways to engage with the collection through its descriptive metadata. Bibliographic descriptions for the materials are available at the Ransom Center Website.
